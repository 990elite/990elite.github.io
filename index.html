Welcome to the Sprinkle Tree Project

About Sprinkle Tree

We saw that many trees were burning from wildfires. We need them because they consume carbon dioxide and they filter the air, so we decided to create SprinkleTree. When heat is detected, SprinkleTree will start spinning and spraying water everywhere.

Our Mission

Our project SprinkleTree will help and stop wildfires from ever happening again

Project Features

  • Feature 1: SprinkleTree will spin its head and will spray water in it's surroundings
  • Feature 2: SprinkleTree can send massages to the local authorities to alert people
  • Feature 3: SprinkleTree is made out of sustainable and recycled materials
